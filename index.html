How does Sonix AI detect emotions and suggest songs? ᐯ

Face Detection in Software:

Face detection technology identifies human faces within images or video frames. It works by:

  • Feature Extraction: Scanning an image or video frame for patterns resembling a face, such as eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Pattern Matching: Comparing identified facial features to a database of known facial patterns to confirm the presence of a face.

Voice Detection through Sonix:

Sonix uses advanced voice detection technology to analyze and interpret spoken words. This process involves:

  • Audio Capture: Recording voice input using a microphone.
  • Speech Recognition: Converting spoken words into text using algorithms that recognize and process human language.
